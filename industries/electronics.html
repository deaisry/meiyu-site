Product Design Support

Engineering Program Managers work comprehensively with the Product Design Team to ensure all requirements are known and understood to smoothly move your project from concept to finished product. Whether you need design assistance for your plastic injection molded components or design assistance during the final stages of refinement prior to building a mold, we can provide you with the design services you need:

• Proficient with all major CAD software in working with your 2D drawings and 3D models.

• Focus on Design for Manufacture ability to optimize all manufacturing and assembly processes to reduce overall cost.

• “Jump-Ahead” product design approach to give your company product differentiation and competitive advantage.

• Experienced in providing design solutions for part consolidation and complex assemblies.

• Wide-range of rapid prototyping capabilities to evaluate form, fit, and function. Prove design concepts and assist you with your marketing needs.

Tooling Design Services

At Meiyu, we understand that your investment in tooling is substantial and the proper design and construction of your mold is critical. As part of the product development process, our experienced Tooling Engineers will work with your Program Manager to ensure that your molds are well-designed and constructed to deliver maximum part-to-part repeatability and reliability over the life of your product.
