Verify and Validate Your Part Design and More

Meiyu is known by major OEMs in many industries for our reputation as a highly-experienced and innovative custom injection molder. We utilize the latest in manufacturing and automation technologies in our 15,000 square meters, purpose-built molding facilities to give you high quality parts at the best possible price. Our Manufacturing Group has extensive molding industry experience and applies the best practices of lean manufacturing principles throughout our operation.

Whether your parts specify tight tolerances, have complex geometry or are highly cosmetic, our technical staff is battle tested and can meet your difficult molding requirements. If your parts require post-molding operations or full contract manufacturing, we offer a complete range of in-house assembly, testing, decorating and packaging services.
