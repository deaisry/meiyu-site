About Us

INNOVATION IN MANUFACTURING, LEADING THE FUTURE!

Dongguan Meiyu Precision Technology Co., LTD is a manufacture enterprise of mold design and manufacturing, plastic injection molding, product R&D, and assembly, painting and silk screen printing of plastic parts. Our products include: power adaptor plastic housing, various plastic cases, electronic cigarette plastic shell, silicone product, automobile plastic product mold, medical equipment, smart homes, plastic accessories for beauty equipment and so on. The company was founded in 2003, covering an area of 14000 square meters, and is located in Hengli Town, Dongguan City, Guangdong Province, with convenient transportation. At present, we have more than 30 professional mold manufacturing equipment, over 70 injection molding equipment, more than 30 automation equipment, 20 painting and assembly equipment, and more than 30 measuring and testing equipment, etc. The company strictly implements the ISO9001, ISO14001 and IATF16949 management systems, and has established a complete set of process control and testing systems, winning the trust and support of customers with high quality, high efficiency and zero return.

The company promises to deal with customer complaints efficiently and ensure customer satisfaction through practical actions; Technical innovation, process optimization and automation to ensure excellence; Ensure 100% on-time delivery rate through continuous improvement of production efficiency. The company is based on the market, constantly enrich and improve itself, strengthen management to survive, expand operation to develop, and wholeheartedly provide customers with satisfactory products and high-quality services. We looking forward to creating a better future with you.
